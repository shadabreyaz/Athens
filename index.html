Who We Are

Where Myth Becomes Memory

Founded in 2022, the Athens Living Museum is a creative theatre company dedicated to bringing Greek culture and history to life through immersive experiences. From our debut Ancient Greek murder mystery “Who Killed Callimachos?” to the culinary journey “Around Greece in Five Dishes” and our latest weaving workshop “Ftou Ftou!”, we blend storytelling, performance, and tradition in unforgettable ways.
